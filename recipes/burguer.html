Burguer

Description

This is the most popular food in America and it's absolutely awesome, I really love it

It's meat within bread and has complements like onion, tomato, lettuce, bacon, etc...

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Lettuce

Steps

  1. Make the bread, or buy it if you're lazy and cock the meat, a big piece
  2. Cut a onion and a tomato
  3. Put onion, tomato, lettuce and the meat within the bread
  4. And thats all, a traditional burguer, enjoy it
