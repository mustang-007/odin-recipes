Pizza

Description

The most popular food in Italia and probably in the entire world, I'm talking about pizza.

It's bread with cheese and pepperoni, that it's not the original way to do it, but still being delicious.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Cheese
  • Pepperoni

Steps

  1. you will need to make the bread, or you can buy it also, but it's not the same
  2. buy some pepperoni and cheese
  3. put on the bread the pepperoni and the cheese, then put the pizza in an oven with the perfect heat
  4. and it's all, enjoy it
